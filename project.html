Discover Kerala - God's Own Country

Kerala is a beautiful state in southern India, known for its lush greenery, serene backwaters, and vibrant culture.Kerala is a state in southwestern India known for its natural beauty, including backwaters, beaches, and mountains, earning it the nickname "God's Own Country". It is also recognized for its rich culture, vibrant festivals, and art forms like Kathakali, and it boasts a high literacy rate. Geographically, the state is located between the Arabian Sea and the Western Ghats and has a tropical climate.
