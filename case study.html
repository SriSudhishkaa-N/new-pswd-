Top Places to Explore

Munnar

Famous for tea plantations, misty hills, and breathtaking viewpoints.

Alleppey

Known for its serene backwaters and traditional houseboat experiences.

Kochi

A vibrant mix of history, culture, beaches, and modern city life.

Must‑Try Kerala Foods

Sadya

A traditional feast served on a banana leaf with over 20 dishes.

Appam & Stew

Soft hoppers paired with a mild and creamy vegetable or chicken stew.

Malabar Biryani

Fragrant, slightly sweet, flavorful biryani from North Kerala.

Culture & Traditions

Kerala is home to classical dance forms like Kathakali and Mohiniyattam, vibrant festivals like Onam and Thrissur Pooram, and centuries‑old Ayurvedic wellness practices. The state blends nature, heritage, and modernity in a unique way.
